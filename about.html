About Me

Passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences through technology and design

My Journey

As a senior Information Science major at Michigan State University, I've discovered my passion lies at the intersection of technology, design, and human experience. My journey began with a curiosity about how systems work and evolved into a deep commitment to creating solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

Through my coursework in systems analysis, web development, and research methodology, I've developed a unique perspective on solving complex problems. I believe that the best solutions come from understanding both the technical requirements and the human needs behind every project.

My approach combines rigorous analytical thinking with creative problem-solving. Whether I'm designing a database schema, conducting user research, or building a responsive website, I focus on creating elegant solutions that are both functional and user-friendly.

What Drives Me

I'm fascinated by the way technology can transform ideas into reality. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new and push the boundaries of what's possible. I thrive in environments where innovation is encouraged and collaboration is valued.

Beyond the code and systems, I'm driven by the impact of my work. Whether it's improving security systems while protecting privacy, combating misinformation online, or making health technology more accessible, I'm committed to projects that matter.
